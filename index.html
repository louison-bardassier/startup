Our founder's profile!

Our founder, Louison Bardassier, was raised in Courchevel, where she learned how to ski and fell in love with the sport. As she didn't like the way ski schools taught, she decided to create her own, where individuals could learn how to ski in a more entertaining and motivating way. First created in Courchevel, The Ski Gaper extended to four locations, offering now more diverse lessons, such as off piste skiing and snowboarding.
